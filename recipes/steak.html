Juicy, Tender Steak

Pre-salted for up to 24 hours and then placed on a screaming hot grill, this recipe will guarantee tender, juicy steaks regardless of your doneness preference.

The secret to a tender steak is pre-salting 24 hours before grilling. The salt will not dry out your protein, in fact, it will liquify and re-asborb back into the beef, ensuring an evenly salt throughout the protein.

Ingredients:

  • A cut of steak of your choice
  • Course kosher salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Pat the steaks dry.
  2. Cover the entire surface with the Kosher Salt.
  3. Place the salted protein into a freezer bag and refigerate for 24 hours.
  4. Remove steak from fridge, cover with garlic powder and pepper.
  5. Heat the left side of the grill to 500F, and the other side on low.
  6. Place steak on the left side, sear for 4 minutes total, turning ever minute.
  7. Place the steak onto the cool side of the grill, and grill to desired temp, turning every minute.
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes, 10 minutes if the steak is thicker and 1 inch.
