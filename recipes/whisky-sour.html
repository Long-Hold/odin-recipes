Whisky Sour

Sweet, Sour, and Chilled. A Whisky Sour Garnished with Cinnamon

This classic Whisky Sour recipe with a twist.

Shaken with your choice of Whisky, this recipe uses spiced syrup, a switch up from the standard simple syrup. This drink is sure to please your tastebuds.

Garnish with a cinnamon stick for an extra touch of style and spice.

Ingredients:

  • 1oz Whisky, Bourbon, or Scotch
  • 3/4oz Lemon Juice
  • 3/4oz Spiced Syrup
