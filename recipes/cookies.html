Decadent, Bakery-Style Chocolate Chip Cookies

These delicious, soft chocolate chip cookies will leave you and your friends not wanting for more.

This recipe utilizes a clever technique to get that bakery-styled look.

The secret to bakery-style cookies is to pull apart the dough balls into two pieces and smoosh them together, and then add more chocolate chips to the cookies after they've baked, while cooling down.

By following the above guidelines, you are certain to achieve perfect cookies everytime.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 11/2 sticks butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 11/2 cups chocolate chips, plus extra
